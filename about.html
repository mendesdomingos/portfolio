Driven by a passion for education and fascinated by technology, my name is Daniel and I am on an exciting journey to combine these two areas in pursuit of a more innovative and effective educational future. My passion for education was solidified during my technical course in School Administration, where I had the opportunity to experience firsthand the challenges and joys of the school environment. This experience awakened in me the desire to contribute to the construction of a more efficient, engaging, and 21st century-ready educational system. With this vision in mind, I decided to enroll in the Information Systems course. In this course, I seek to acquire the technical and practical knowledge necessary to develop innovative solutions that enable the transformation of education. I believe that technology has enormous potential to make learning more dynamic, personalized, and accessible to everyone.
